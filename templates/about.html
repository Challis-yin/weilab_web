Principal Investigator

Personal Profile
Leyi Wei received the B.Sc. degree in Computing Mathematics from Xiamen University in 2010, the M.E. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University in 2013 and in 2016, respectively. During 2016 - 2019, he worked as an Assistant Professor at College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. He also worked as a project researcher in the lab of Functional analysis in silico, Institute of Medical Science, University of Tokyo during 2018 - 2019. Since 2020.01 till now, he is a Professor at School of Software, Shandong University.
